About the Author

David Meyers

Pittsburgh · Allderdice High, '72

David Meyers is the founder and CEO of a nationally recognized environmental consulting firm working in the public interest. He grew up playing pickup and high-school basketball in Pittsburgh, which inspired the stories and characters in the book. The author played for the '69 Allderdice Dragons.
