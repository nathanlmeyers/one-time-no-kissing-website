What readers are saying.

One Time, No Kissing had me turning pages, smiling. A remarkable, funny, poignant, and heartfelt story of a high-school basketball team — diverse students all somehow getting along, getting a good education, forming their own opinions. The sport, the passion for it, and the execution of the game is a character all by itself.

If you came of age in the later 1960s and early '70s amid the turbulence of Vietnam, the antiwar movement, and the demands for civil rights and justice — this book will resonate deeply. For any thoughtful reader, Meyers offers a spellbinding story that is vibrant and alive, and deeply moving.

About the Author

David Meyers

Pittsburgh · Allderdice High, '72

David Meyers is the founder and CEO of a nationally recognized environmental consulting firm working in the public interest. He grew up playing pickup and high-school basketball in Pittsburgh, which inspired the stories and characters in the book. The author played for the '69 Allderdice Dragons.
